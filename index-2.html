Provenance

Daniel Deepak is a growth marketing consultant for B2B, B2C, SaaS, and service brands — turning brand strategy into measurable demand through positioning, paid media, and go-to-market. What sets the practice apart is the engineering: rather than only using AI, the work includes building it — open-source Claude Skills and MCP servers that automate the senior parts of growth.

The works in this collection span paid search, SEO, lifecycle, and analytics across retail, marketplace, and service categories — each documented to the standard a client or recruiter can click into and verify, not merely read about.
